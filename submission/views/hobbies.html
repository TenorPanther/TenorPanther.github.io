My Hobbies

Music

I have been playing the trumpet for 9 years and am a second-year member of the University of Colorado Golden Buffalo Marching Band. I love setting the gameday atmosphere and performing for the sports fans. I can also play some piano and clarinet.

Writing

In my spare time, I write short stories. I like fleshing out fictional worlds, riffing on the ideas of my favorite stories, and sometimes, just writing for the sake of it! I typically write science fiction and fantasy but have branched out into other genres.

Gaming

I enjoy the rich storytelling gaming has to offer as a medium. I prefer story-driven games such as Red Dead Redemption 2 (depicted above) and Persona 4 and 5 but also appreciate the varied experiences in Minecraft, Skyrim, and Project Zomboid.
